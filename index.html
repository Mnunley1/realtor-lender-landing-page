Just a little about me

Hello, my name is Jacqueline (Jackie) Nunley aka Loan Diva and aka Track Mom. I'm a long time resident of Garner, NC, a US Navy veteran, wife of 1 patient man, mother of 4 athletically talented children, huge track&field sports fan and for 20+ years I have been specializing in helping homebuyers obtain the American Dream of Homeownership. My goal with every Loan is to Deliver Immediate Valuable Attention to the borrower. I am especially passionate about helping 1st time homebuyers and credit challenged buyers make their dreams come true. Prior to starting my mortgage career I specialized in onsite new home sales for 6 years. My experience in both the real estate and mortgage industry gives me the ability to understand and relate to all aspects of the mortgage experience in purchasing a home. I can help borrowers by analyzing the their financial status to determine the best possible mortgage loan product to meet their financial needs. I believe that borrowers should know what to expect and understand the whole process when applying for a mortgage loan. I will spend time discussing the details of their financing options with them, ensuring that their are no surprises at closing. I also believe that everyone deserves a chance at homeownership despite past problems.
